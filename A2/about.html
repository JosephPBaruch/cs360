About

I have experience in various programming languages including JavaScript, TypeScript (React), Go, Python, C, and C++. I am also proficient with developer tools such as Docker, Git, Kubernetes, Unix, and AWS.

In addition to my work experience, I have been involved in various projects such as Kvis: Kubernetes Visualizer, josephbaruch.com, and game development projects using C# and Unity. I have also trained a climate image classification model using Python.
